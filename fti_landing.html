The Framework

Five Plans.
Run Simultaneously.

The core innovation of Fast Track: you don't pick one path and hope. You build all five in parallel, so every plan acts as a fail-safe for the one above it.

A
BS/MD & BA/MD

Direct-entry combined programs from high school. Highest reward, worth pursuing from day one.

Undergraduate EAPs that provide a guaranteed medical school seat before you graduate.

3-year medical school programs with direct residency pipelines. Not limited to primary care.

Common Questions

FAQ

Who is this course for?
Fast Track Intensive is built for high school students (grades 9–12), college freshmen and sophomores, and parents of premeds who want a strategic, execution-focused roadmap to medicine. If you're looking for motivation, this isn't it. If you want a real system, this is it.
Is this only for students who want to do a BS/MD program?
No. The 5-plan framework covers BS/MD programs, Early Assurance Pathways, 3-year undergraduate acceleration, CAMPP 3-year medical schools, and the traditional optimized pathway. You run all five simultaneously — so no matter what happens, you're positioned well.
What if I'm already in college?
The course is still highly relevant for early college students (freshman and sophomore year). Several plans — including EAPs, 3-year undergrad compression, and CAMPP schools — are still accessible. The earlier you find this, the more options you have.
How is this different from generic premed advice?
Generic premed advice tells you to get a good GPA and study for the MCAT. Fast Track Intensive teaches you how to run five parallel pathways simultaneously, compress your undergraduate timeline, shift your timeline so you can graduate sooner. It's built by a physician who actually did it — not someone who read about it.
How do I access the course after purchase?
After purchase you'll receive immediate access to all course materials digitally. The modules are pre-recorded and self-paced — you can go through them on your own schedule.
